Book Summary and Reflection


In "Twelve Pillars" by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener, the authors delve into essential principles for personal growth and success. The chapters I've read (Chapters 4 â€“ 7) have provided valuable insights into achieving one's goals and improving the quality of life.

One of the key takeaways from these chapters is the concept of setting and pursuing meaningful goals. Rohn and Widener emphasize that setting a goal is not merely about the end result but about the personal growth and development that occur during the journey to achieve that goal.

The authors highlight the importance of consistency and discipline in working towards our goals. They stress that writing down our goals is a crucial step because it brings them into reality and allows our minds to focus on achieving them.

Personally, I found this reading to be incredibly motivating. It has made me reevaluate my approach to goal setting and life planning. I now understand that each goal I set should serve as a catalyst for personal growth, making me a better, more capable individual along the way.

Moving forward, I intend to apply the principles from "Twelve Pillars" to my life by setting clear, written goals and embracing the idea that the pursuit of these goals will shape me into a better person. This book has instilled in me a renewed sense of purpose and determination to achieve my dreams.

Degree Pursuit and Top Skills


Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Computer Information Systems. This academic journey aligns with my personal and professional goals, as it equips me with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in my chosen field.


As for my top three skills acquired so far, they include:


  1. Critical Thinking: This skill empowers me to approach challenges and decisions with a discerning eye. I've learned to analyze information, consider various perspectives, and make informed choices.
  2. Database Management: Database Management: A strong understanding of database management systems (DBMS) is fundamental in data analytics. Skills in designing, querying, and maintaining relational databases using SQL (Structured Query Language) are highly valuable.
  3. Data Visualization: Data Visualization: Data visualization is the skill of presenting complex data in a visual format, such as charts, graphs, and dashboards. Proficiency in data visualization tools and techniques allows you to communicate data-driven insights effectively to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

These skills are integral to my growth and will play a significant role in achieving the success I aspire to in my academic and professional life.
